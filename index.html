Dancing with the Stars: The Mirrorball's Destiny《与星共舞》数学建模

2026 Mathematical Contest In Modeling (MCM) | 01/2026 - 02/20262026年美国大学生数学建模竞赛 (MCM) | 2026年1月 - 2026年2月

+

Investigating the conflict between professional elitism and mass public appeal in reality TV voting systems.探究真人秀投票机制中，专业评委与大众观众的娱乐偏好之间常常存在冲突。

Overview & Background概述与背景

Context背景

The show "Dancing with the Stars" struggles with the conflict between expert technical scoring and subjective fan voting, often leading to controversial elimination results.《与星共舞》节目面临着专家技术打分与粉丝主观投票之间的冲突，因此往往会引发出具有争议的淘汰结果。

Objective目标

To develop a mathematical model that estimates hidden fan voting data, evaluates past scoring rules, and proposes a fairer voting mechanism.建立数学模型来估算隐藏的粉丝投票数据，来评估过去的评分规则，并提出一个更公平的投票机制。

Role角色

As the core modeler on the team, I led the design and code implementation of the underlying algorithmic framework. I was responsible for building a vote estimation model using Monte Carlo algorithms and translating complex audience psychology into quantifiable, interpretable mathematical features through Random Forest and SHAP attribution analysis in machine learning.作为团队中的核心建模手，我主导底层算法框架的设计与代码实现。负责运用蒙特卡洛算法构建投票估算模型，并通过机器学习中的随机森林与SHAP归因分析，将复杂的受众心理转化为可量化、可解释的数学特征。

Core Methodologies核心方法论

  • PL-PECON: Used Monte Carlo rejection sampling to decode latent fan preferences, achieving a 96.73% historical consistency rate.使用蒙特卡洛拒绝采样解码潜在的粉丝偏好，并实现96.73%的历史一致性。
  • PRIME-AMC: Used Markov Chains to evaluate and contrast the scoring effects of ranking versus percentage methods.使用马尔可夫链来评估与对比排名制与百分比制的打分效果。
  • DSRF-SHAP: Combined SHAP with a Dual-Stream Random Forest model to decouple voting drivers.结合 SHAP 的双流随机森林模型，解耦投票驱动因素。
  • SARF: A Sigmoid-gated adaptive rank fusion that conditionally activates fan votes based on technical thresholds.基于技术阈值条件激活粉丝投票的Sigmoid门控自适应排名融合。

Research Findings研究结论

Key Findings核心发现

When a percentage-based voting method is adopted, there is a severe bias towards fan votes. Conversely, the ranking method effectively suppresses extreme variance and maintains a high signal-to-noise ratio of 0.96.当采取百分制投票时，粉丝投票倾向严重，而排名制能有效抑制极端方差，并保持 0.96 的高信噪比。

Strategic Recommendation策略建议

Recommended a hybrid strategy combining the ranking method with a "Judges' Save" mechanism, supplemented by dynamic gating, to ensure the trophy truly represents dancing excellence.建议采取混合策略，即排名制+评委保障机制，并辅以动态门控，以确保奖杯真正代表卓越的舞蹈水平。

Hainan Tourism Research: Predicting High-Engagement Guides海南旅游研究：高点赞旅游攻略预测

2025 "Academic Excellence Climbing Program" | 11/20252025年"学术攀登计划" | 2025年11月

+

Revealing the core characteristics of highly-liked user travel guides on Qunar.com through data-driven analysis.基于数据驱动分析，揭示去哪儿网上高赞用户旅游攻略的核心特征。

Overview & Background概述与背景

Context背景

Although highly-liked travel guides on the internet profoundly influence modern travel planning, it is difficult for both content creators and platforms to accurately identify what factors drive this high engagement.尽管网络上高点赞旅游攻略深刻影响着现代出行计划，但内容创作者和平台方都很难精准识别究竟是什么因素驱动了这些高互动量。

Objective目标

To build predictive models using machine learning and natural language processing techniques, identify key drivers of "likes," and provide actionable strategies.运用机器学习和自然语言处理技术构建预测模型，识别点赞的关键因素，并提供可落地的策略。

Role角色

As the lead data analyst, I independently completed the entire data mining process, from unstructured text processing to feature engineering. I proficiently utilized NLP techniques to clean and parse massive amounts of text, and built Decision Tree and Quantile Regression models to transform discrete text content into predictive metrics.作为数据分析主力，我独立完成了从非结构化文本处理到特征工程的全流程数据挖掘。熟练运用 NLP 技术清洗并解析海量文本，并构建决策树与分位数回归模型，将离散的文本内容转化为预测指标。

Core Methodologies核心方法论

  • Semantic Analysis & NLP: Applied text mining and LDA topic modeling to extract structured features from unstructured travelogues.运用文本挖掘和LDA主题模型，从非结构化的游记攻略中提取结构化特征。
  • Quantile Regression: Analyzed the non-linear impact of per capita consumption, revealing a negative correlation in low-to-medium engagement content, but an insignificant impact in top-tier, highly-liked content.分析人均消费的非线性影响，揭示其在中低互动量内容中呈负相关，但在头部高赞内容中影响不显著。
  • Decision Tree Classification: Built and optimized a classifier using grid search to predict the likelihood of high engagement based on dimensions such as travel group type, attractions, and number of photos.构建并使用网格搜索优化分类器，基于出行群体类型、景点及照片数量等维度来预测高互动的可能性。

Research Findings研究结论

Key Findings核心发现

"Shopping" (0.550) and "Food" (0.160) are the strongest predictors, with weights far exceeding the mere mention of popular beaches. Furthermore, travelogues featuring "family" or "friends" traveling together have higher engagement rates."购物"(0.550)和"美食"(0.160)是最强的预测指标，其权重远超单纯提及热门海滩。此外，"家庭"或"朋友"结伴出行的游记互动率更高。

Strategic Recommendation策略建议

Formulated a content creation formula (e.g., combining in-depth tours, food, and photography for group travel) to maximize engagement rates and optimize the platform's recommendation algorithms.总结出内容创作公式（例如：针对团队游，将深度游、美食与摄影相结合），以最大化互动率并优化平台的推荐算法。

Zhejiang Entrepreneurship & Organizational Resilience项目三：浙商精神抽样调查研究

ZJSU College Students Innovation & Entrepreneurship Training Program | 04/2025 - Present浙江工商大学大学生创新创业训练计划 | 2025年4月 - 至今

+

Employed a mixed-methods approach, utilizing survey data from 264 startups and in-depth qualitative interviews, to deeply explore how the "Zhejiang Spirit" empowers organizational resilience in enterprises.采用问卷调查与深度访谈的混合研究方法，基于264家企业的实证数据，深入探讨"浙商精神"这一地域商业文化如何赋能初创企业的组织韧性。

Overview & Background概述与背景

Context背景

Amidst a complex macroeconomic environment, existing research primarily focuses on formal institutional support, rarely addressing the profound impact of informal cultural systems—such as regional business spirit—on a company's ability to withstand risks.当前复杂严峻的宏观经济环境下，现有研究多聚焦于正式制度支持，鲜少关注地域文化（如浙商精神）这一非正式制度对企业抗风险能力的深层影响。

Objective目标

To decode the core essence of the Zhejiang Spirit, unveil its internal mechanisms for enhancing entrepreneurial resilience through Structural Equation Modeling, and distill a replicable "Zhejiang Experience" to guide entrepreneurial practices nationwide.剖析"浙商精神"的核心内涵，通过构建结构方程模型揭示其提升创业韧性的内在作用机理，并提炼浙江经验，为全国创新创业提供理论支撑与实践指导。

Role角色

I was deeply involved in field research and data analysis. I performed multi-level coding on 60,000 words of in-depth interview transcripts, achieving a perfect integration of data models and business narratives.我深度参与了实地调研与数据分析，对6万字的深度访谈记录进行多级编码，实现了数据模型与商业叙事的完美结合。

Core Methodologies核心方法论

  • Empirical Analysis: Conducted a questionnaire survey of 264 startups. Used Structural Equation Modeling (SEM) to quantify impact paths, and applied Importance-Performance Analysis (IPA) to process interview texts.对共264家初创企业进行问卷调查。使用结构方程模型 (SEM) 量化影响路径，并运用重要性-表现分析法 (IPA) 处理访谈文本。
  • Longitudinal Case Study: Conducted an in-depth analysis of Hangang Group's 10-year transition journey, constructing the APCC (Antecedents-Process-Consequences-Chinese Context) model.深度剖析杭钢集团 10 年的转型历程，构建了APCC（前因-过程-结果-中国情境）模型。
  • Field Research: Conducted over 5 hours of in-depth interviews, compiled 60,000 words of verbatim transcripts, and used multi-level coding for qualitative comprehensive analysis.开展5小时以上的深度访谈，整理出6万字逐字稿，使用多级编码进行定性综合分析。

Research Findings研究结论

Key Findings核心发现

Validated that regional spirit can enhance corporate resilience by stimulating a willingness to take risks, promoting cooperation, alleviating financing constraints, and strengthening a sense of responsibility.验证了地域精神能够通过激发风险承担意愿、促进合作、缓解融资约束以及强化责任感来提升企业韧性。

Strategic Recommendation策略建议

Proposed a synergistic strategy combining an "efficient market" with a "proactive government" for policymakers, providing a roadmap for the transformation of the traditional manufacturing industry.为政策制定者提出了将"有效市场"与"有为政府"相结合的协同策略，为传统制造业转型提供路线图。
